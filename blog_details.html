Our practice is grounded in a set of core values that guide every interaction and counselling session. We prioritize compassion, creating a judgment-free space where you can feel safe and understood. Respect for your unique journey and experiences is at the heart of what we do, and we approach our work with empathy, recognizing the courage it takes to seek help and make positive changes in your life.

At True Guidance Hub, your well-being is our top priority. We are committed to providing you with the highest standard of care, and we take your trust in us seriously. We will work tirelessly to support you on your journey to personal growth, healing, and happiness. Dr. David

1. Remember not to sweat the small stuff

You can message your therapist at anytime, from anywhere. You also get to schedule live sessions when it's convenient for you, and can connect from any mobile device or computer.

2. Practice Acceptance

Talk to your therapist however you feel comfortable — text, chat, phone, or video.

  • Find solutions instead of complaining
  • Practice letting go
  • Let beauty in

3. Exercise patience

It's a reminder to take a step back, breathe, and approach things with a calm and understanding attitude. Practicing patience can help improve relationships, reduce stress, and lead to better decision-making.

4. Lower your expectations

Lowering expectations can be a helpful mindset in managing disappointments and reducing stress. It involves setting more realistic or achievable goals, which can lead to a greater sense of satisfaction when those goals are met. It's about finding a balance between aiming high and being realistic, which can help in maintaining a positive outlook and resilience in the face of challenges.

5. Remember you both desire harmony

It's important to remember that you value harmony in your interactions with others. This can guide your approach in situations where patience and managing expectations play a role, helping you maintain a balanced and considerate perspective.
